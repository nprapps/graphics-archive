How Parents Perceive Social Media’s Effect On Children

Among parents whose child has a social media account

Outcome
Helps
Hurts

Emotional well-being 18% 20% 62%

Note: Percentages may not add up to 100 due to rounding.

Source: Common Sense Media: “The common sense census: Plugged-in parents of tweens and teens” (Dec. 2016). Based on a survey of 1,786 parents of children age 8 to 18 living in the United States, conducted from July 8 to July 25, 2016.
