In Connecticut, Disparities In Who Gets Tased, Warned

Notes

— The study’s authors evaluated data from Connecticut’s police departments for 2015. They noted that Taser warnings are likely undercounted, due in part to inconsistent reporting practices.
— A warning includes “laser sight only, warning arc or removing the taser from the holster. Electric shock was not administered in any of these circumstances.”

Source: Institute for Municipal and Regional Policy at Central Connecticut State University
