How To Win An Election With 27 Percent Of The Vote

The below 11 states, combined, would give a candidate 270 electoral votes — just over 50 percent of that total and, therefore, enough to win the election (given a two-person race). However, they also together made up only around 27 percent of the popular vote in the 2012 election.
