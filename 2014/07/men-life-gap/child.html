The Life Expectancy Gap (1929-2010)

Males born in 2010 have an estimated life expectancy of 76.2 years, compared with 81.0 years for females — a difference of 4.8 years. The gap was largest in 1975 and 1978, at an estimated 7.8 fewer years of life for boys born those years.

Source: National Vital Statistics System (CDC)
