1. The California State Fair is underway, running until July 27. Which of these foods is actually served there?

  • Double-fried honey cicadas, presented on a leaf-shaped funnel cake
  • A cheeseburger stuffed with macaroni and cheese on a Krispy Kreme bun The dish is a riff on the "Luther Burger," a bacon cheeseburger served on a Krispy Kreme doughnut bun that is named for R&B singer Luther Vandross.
  • Sriracha-flavored jumbo gummy worm on a stick

2. At the Indiana State Fair in August, which of these items will actually be served?

  • A hot beef sundae layered with mashed potatoes, marinated beef, gravy, cheese, corn "sprinkles" and topped with a cherry tomato. This play on a hot fudge ice cream sundae debuted at the Iowa State Fair in 2006, according to the Chicago Tribune.
  • Vegan surprise: a tofu and kale salad, with a hidden cube of calf liver inside.
  • Beer-battered turkey drumsticks drizzled with fudge

3. In Minnesota, fairgoers can enjoy which of the following treats in late August?

  • Grilled rattlesnake on a stick, with toasted skin served separately.
  • Dessert Walleye, dusted with cinnamon sugar, fried and paired with a jello salad.
  • Dessert Salami: Chocolate, butter, almonds and walnuts rolled into a salami shape and sliced. This is the first year dessert salami will be available at the Minnesota State Fair. Other new items include beer gelato, deep-fried breakfast on-a-stick, gluten-free beer-battered brat, schnitzel strips and jello salad ice cream.
