Presidential Visits By The Numbers

This chart shows the number of days during Obama's presidency he has spent in a state with at least one public appearance, be that at a fundraiser, a campaign rally or local ice cream stand. If it made news or is in the public papers of the President, it's here. He tends to most visit states where he is popular (fundraising, speeches) and swing states (campaigning).
