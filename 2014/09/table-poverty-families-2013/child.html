Share Of Families Below The Poverty Level In 2013

All families 11.2%
Married couple 5.8%
Female householder, no husband present 30.6%
Male householder, no wife present 15.9%

Notes

The Census Bureau defines a family as: “two or more people (one of whom is the householder) related by birth, marriage, or adoption residing in the same housing unit.”
