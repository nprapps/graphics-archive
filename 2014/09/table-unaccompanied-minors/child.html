Counties Receiving Unaccompanied Children

The Office of Refugee Resettlement releases children to sponsors (usually, family members) while their immigration cases are under review. Below, counties receiving unaccompanied children between January and July 2014.

Top 10 Counties Nationwide

Harris County, Texas 2,866 children
Los Angeles County, Calif. 1,993 children
Suffolk County, N.Y. 1,181 children
Miami-Dade County, Fla. 1,127 children
Nassau County, N.Y. 1,096 children
Fairfax County, Va. 1,023 children
Prince George's County, Md. 960 children
Dallas County, Texas 851 children
Montgomery County, Md. 816 children
Palm Beach County, Fla. 785 children

Counties In Louisiana

Jefferson Parish, La. 533 children
Orleans Parish, La. 237 children
East Baton Rouge Parish, La. 173 children
St. Tammany Parish, La. 54 children
Lafayette Parish, La. 51 children

Notes

Data as of July 31, 2014. Data released only for counties that received 50 or more children (27,706 of 37,477 total children).
