Protection Of Family Bank Accounts

Every state gives creditors the right to seize funds from a debtorâ€™s bank account once they have obtained a court judgment. But state law varies on how much a creditor can take from the account. Several states set a fixed amount that the creditor cannot touch. However, many states provide little to no protections.
