All Of The Sweets

  • Buffalo Sponge

    Like this caramelized sugar candy from upstate New York, you're just a bit mysterious. As people get to know you, they quickly realize your smooth exterior hides an unexpectedly complex character.

  • Manju

    Like these traditional sweets from Japan — by way of Seattle — you're a world traveler. You're sweet but never treacly, and you have a subtle, unique charm that sets you apart from the crowd.

  • Leche Quemada

    Like this Texas-Mexico border candy made of burnt milk and pecans, you can be a bit rough around the edges. Yet you have a rustic, earthy quality that people can't resist.

  • Modjeska

    Like this caramel-marshmallow dumpling from Louisville, Ky., you're a package of sweet and light. With your bouncy personality, you're a true people-magnet, and if something gets you down, you bounce right back again.

  • Chile Brittle

    Like this New Mexico scorcher, you deliver a lively kick. For some mild-mannered folks, your smoky, smoldering company is best enjoyed in small doses, but people who like to play with fire can't get enough of you.

  • GooGoo Cluster

    Like the classic Tennessee treat, folks love it when you show up at a party. You're popular, a little bit nutty, and people often turn to you for comfort when they're feeling a little low.

  • Chukar Cherry

    Like these chocolate-covered Washington State cherries, you're the perfect balance of tart and sweet. You're easily at home at a sophisticated cocktail bar, and you wouldn't dream of going to a party without the perfect bottle of red wine.

  • Valomilk

    Like this Kansas classic, you are anything but subtle. As sweet as can be, you're full of youthful energy. At a party, people will know you instantly — you're the one dancing on the table.
