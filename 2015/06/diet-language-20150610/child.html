Plastics Are Not Fantastic

The dangers of plastics are no surprise to scientists, who have been studying their negative health effects for decades. You may have heard about the most notorious of these plastics, BPA. Polycarbonate plastics (containing BPA) are extremely common in US food packaging. In 2004, the CDC found traces of BPA in nearly all the urine samples it collected (93 percent of adults), and scientists predict that today the number would be over 99 percent. The source of this BPA is our diets. Seems alarming, but there are traces of many things in our bodies that are only dangerous at high doses. The dose makes the poison.
