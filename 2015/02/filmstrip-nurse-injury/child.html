Lifting A Patient From A Bed

NPR correspondent Daniel Zwerdling donned sensing equipment while practicing lifting a “patient” from a bed. The image on the right is a simulation of the stresses on his spine during that process. The length and direction of the arrows indicate the magnitude and distribution of forces. The color intensity signals the total amount of stress on the discs in his spine.
