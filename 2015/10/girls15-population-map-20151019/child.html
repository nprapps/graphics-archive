Half Of The World's Teen Girls Live In 7 Countries

 A third of all girls age 10 to 19 live in India and China. Throughout this story we’ll highlight those two countries in orange.
The remaining five countries — Brazil, Indonesia, Nigeria, Pakistan and the United States — bring the total population represented up to just over 50 percent of all teenage girls.

Source: World Bank Population Estimates for 2015
