Level Of Key Fertility Hormone Declines With Age

A 2010 study of 17,210 female patients at fertility clinics looked at levels of anti-Müllerian hormone (AMH), which is associated with egg supply. AMH is produced by the follicles in the ovaries where the eggs grow and peaks in the mid-20s. While measured levels can vary greatly from woman to woman within the same age range — accounting for the difference between the median and the mean results shown here — researchers noted a general downward trend with age.
